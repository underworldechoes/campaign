The Plan

What I Will Fix

🔌
Classroom Upgrades
Plug points installed in every classroom. Smartboard support extended across all majors — not just select programmes. Your tools should work, everywhere.
📅
Exam Buffer Rule
No examinations — including quizzes — shall be scheduled during the one week before and after Major Fest dates. Your energy, protected.
📊
Clash-Free Timetables
A dedicated scheduling audit to ensure no two exams fall on the same day for any student — regardless of elective combinations.
🛠️
Skill-Focused Learning
Replace repetitive, low-value assignments with practical workshops tailored to Tech, Management, and Law streams. Work that builds your career.
🎓
Attendance Leniency
Structured flexibility for students in internships, portfolio-building, or placement prep — tied to CGPA thresholds so it rewards serious effort, not shortcuts.
🏟️
Court & Lighting Access
Fair, transparent allocation of grounds and courts. Proper lighting installed for evening practice — athletes should not be forced to train in the dark.
🥇
Athlete Leniency
Attendance flexibility for athletes in the lead-up to major tournaments, tied to the same CGPA standards as academic leniency. Performance rewarded fairly.
💻
Digital Reimbursements
Digitise the entire reimbursement workflow — upload bills, track status, receive approvals online. No more chasing paper trails across offices.
📖
Event Legacy Playbooks
A structured handover document after every major event. Each new batch inherits institutional knowledge instead of starting from scratch every year.
📶
Wi-Fi Dead Zones
A full mapping exercise to identify and eliminate dead zones across all hostels and campus buildings. Reliable internet is not a luxury — it's a necessity.
🔧
Maintenance Tracking
A weekly visible tracker for all hostel maintenance requests. Broken fixtures resolved in days, not weeks. Accountability through transparency.
🥗
Mess Quality
More variety, better protein options, fresh fruits daily. The mess should support student health — or fewer dishes done right. Quality over quantity.
